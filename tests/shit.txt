--------------------------------------NORMAL TEXT--------------------------------------
Corteva, Inc. is a global provider of seed and crop protection solutions focused on the agriculture industry. 
Its seed segment is engaged in developing and supplying germplasm and traits that produce optimum yields for farms around the world. 
This segment offers trait technologies that improve resistance to weather, disease, insects and herbicides used to control weeds, 
and trait technologies that enhance food and nutritional characteristics, and provides digital solutions that assist farmers in decision-making. 
Its crop protection segment serves the global agricultural input industry with products that protect against weeds, insects and other pests, 
and disease, and that improve overall crop health both above and below ground through nitrogen management and seed-applied technologies. 
This segment provides herbicides, insecticides, nitrogen stabilizers and pasture and range management herbicides. 
Its portfolio of brands includes CLOSER, DELEGATE, Pioneer, Brevant seeds and others.
